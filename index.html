About SVVVD

A BRAND IN MOTION

SVVVD isn’t a master plan. It’s just someone following a feeling and seeing how far it goes.

DROP 001 is the starting point. Not because it’s perfect, but because it exists. Limited pieces, one clear direction, no overthinking.

22, the stripes, the mesh — not random, not that deep either. Just things that made sense at the time and still do. It’s not nostalgia, it’s not futuristic, it’s just… now.

SVVVD doesn’t stick to one style. If it fits, it stays. If it doesn’t, it’s gone. Simple as that.

No big philosophy. Just doing what feels right, until it doesn’t.
